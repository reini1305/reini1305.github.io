Christian Reinbacher's Pebble apps

After nearly 2 years of Pebble development, it is now time to make this page, to give you an overview over my portfolio of Pebble apps and watchfaces. I'm astonished how active I've been so far :) All apps are open source and therefore available on Github.

If you like what I'm doing and want to support me, consider a donation:

Drink Counter

Drink Counter allows you to keep track of your alcohol, water and cigarette consumption. It even estimates the blood alcohol level based on your gender, weight and number of drinks. NEW: Timeline support (logs your drinks to timeline).
[App store link] [Source link]

Alarms++

With Alarms++ you can flexibly schedule up to 16 (32 on Pebble Time) alarms on specific weekdays. You could set alarms for your work days and another one for the weekend. You can add descriptions to the alarms which will be shown when the alarm goes off. With snooze function, localization and 12h/24h support. NEW: Timeline support!
[App store link] [Source link]

QR Generator

Displays your business card or any string as QR code on your watch! Simply set the string on the configuration page on the phone. The QR code is generated on your phone and sent to the watch. No companion app needed! Works without internet connection.
[App store link] [Source link]

Crazy Eyes

Crazy Eyes shows you the time in a unique way. The left eye represents the hour, the right eye the current minute. The eyebrows are configurable and encode the battery level of your pebble. On a bluetooth disconnect, the eyebrows become narrower. Background color changes randomly every minute. Optionally, the face can be completed with a mouth that shows the day of the week.
[App store link] [Source link]

Fireworks

Fireworks on your Pebble every minute! Shake to firework! (Both configurable of course).
[App store link] [Source link]

Gradient Weather

Minimal watchface that shows you the current condition and 3h forecast in textual form. The background gradient changes according to the conditions. Inspired by the ustwo Smart Watch Faces (especially Episode) for Android Wear.
[App store link] [Source link]

Digilog

Simple combination of analog and digital watchface. The hours are shown as number in the background whereas the minutes are shown in an analog style. You can freely set the colors with a live preview on your phone! Design credits go to /u/thenextbigthing2.
[App store link] [Source link]

Fun with Flags

Learn some new flags on the go! Every minute a new flag is shown. if you flick your wrist, your watch will tell you which country the flag belongs to.
